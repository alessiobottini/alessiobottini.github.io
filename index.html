I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bonn, working in the group of Daniel Huybrechts. I received my Ph.D. in November 2023 from Université Paris-Saclay and Università di Roma "Tor Vergata". My advisors were Emanuele Macrì and Antonio Rapagnetta. My research focuses on the applications of homological algebra to algebraic geometry. Currently I study sheaves on hyper-Kähler manifolds and their moduli spaces.
