I am a postdoc at the University of Bonn, working in the group of Daniel Huybrechts. I received my Ph.D. in November 2023 from Université Paris-Saclay and Tor Vergata University under the supervision of Emanuele Macrì and Antonio Rapagnetta. Here is my CV.

I am broadly interested in the applications of homological algebra to algebraic geometry. Currently, I study sheaves on hyper-Kähler manifolds and their moduli spaces.
